Developed in association with the University of Toronto TETRA society and several of its members. The Concert For The Deaf is an initiative to enable the deaf and hard of hearing to experience music.

We developed a small box that converts audio signals to tactile vibrations. The version 1 prototype was constructed during the LinkedIn Toronto Hackday 2013 and recieved 3rd place.

The version 2 was showcased at the Concert For The Deaf in April 2013.
