Overview

Tile Titans is an HTML5 game that I developed as a side project over the summer of 2013. It is a turn based board game that plays like a mix between chess and tic-tac-toe.

Skills

  • Python
  • Fast Fourier Transform
  • Hi/Lo Pass Filters

Key Learnings

When developing Tile Titans I spent too much time developing non-gameplay features such as a store for purchasing additional tiles and a way to add and challenge friends. I should have only focussed on developing the the core game and a simple matchmaking system and then developed the other features after launching it on Kongregate.

Version 1

Developed in association with the University of Toronto TETRA society and several of its members. The Concert For The Deaf is an initiative to enable the deaf and hard of hearing to experience music.

We developed a small box that converts audio signals to tactile vibrations. The version 1 prototype was constructed during the LinkedIn Toronto Hackday 2013 and received 3rd place.

Version 2

We developed a V2 with improved actuators and a more accurate response. The V2 was showcased at the Concert For The Deaf in March 2013.
